What I Love Doing

Outside of academics and healthcare, I have a variety of interests that keep me energized and inspired. Whether it’s hitting the slopes during winter or enjoying music at a lively concert, I find joy in balancing my busy schedule with meaningful hobbies. These activities not only help me unwind but also provide valuable experiences that influence my personal growth. Some of my favorite hobbies include:

My Favorite Activities

  • Skiing and Ice Skating in the Winter: There's nothing quite like the thrill of gliding down a mountain or skating across a rink on a crisp winter day. Skiing has been a favorite winter sport of mine for years, offering an exhilarating challenge and a perfect escape into nature.
  • Running Along the Charles: Running has become an essential part of my routine, particularly along the scenic Charles River. It’s the perfect way to clear my mind and stay active, offering both physical and mental benefits as I enjoy the beauty of the river and the surrounding city.
  • Playing Tennis with Friends: Tennis is a fun and competitive way to stay fit and connect with friends. Whether it's a casual match or a more competitive game, I love the energy and camaraderie that come with playing this sport.
  • Attending Live Music Events and Concerts: There’s something magical about live music. Whether it’s a small intimate performance or a large-scale concert, I enjoy the opportunity to experience new artists and genres in person.
  • Practicing Mindfulness and Meditation: In the midst of a busy schedule, practicing mindfulness and meditation allows me to stay grounded. These practices help me manage stress and enhance my focus, benefiting both my personal life and academics.
  • Traveling to Explore New Cultures and Cuisines: Traveling opens my eyes to new perspectives, and I love discovering different cultures and their cuisines. Whether it’s a weekend getaway or a longer trip, I relish the opportunity to learn more about the world and expand my horizons.

These activities bring balance to my life, helping me stay motivated and inspired in both my personal and professional pursuits.
